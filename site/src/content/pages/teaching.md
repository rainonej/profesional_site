I approach teaching as a practice of co-inquiry. My role as an instructor is not to transmit a fixed body of knowledge but to create conditions under which students can develop as disciplinary thinkers and ask generative questions.

I believe that rigor and access are not in tension — that the highest intellectual expectations paired with genuine relational support produce the deepest learning. This commitment shapes how I design courses, respond to student work, and build classroom community.
