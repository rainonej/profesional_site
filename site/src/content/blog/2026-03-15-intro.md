Welcome — and thank you for visiting. This site is a placeholder representing the professional presence of Agreni Batra, an educator and researcher whose work sits at the intersection of equitable STEM learning, curriculum design, and teacher preparation. All content on this site is illustrative and will be updated with real information before launch.

The intention of this space is to create a home for ongoing thinking about education, equity, and pedagogy. Rather than a polished portfolio that freezes work in time, this is meant to be a living record — a place to share work in progress, reflect on teaching practice, and engage with colleagues, collaborators, and anyone interested in these questions.

In the months ahead, you can expect writing on curriculum design, notes from classroom practice, and reflections on what it means to build learning environments that center the knowledge and experiences students bring with them. If any of this resonates with your own work, reach out through the contact page — there is a link to book a short conversation directly.
