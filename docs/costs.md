## Scenarios

### Demo-only (current state)

Near-zero cost. Everything runs on free tiers. No custom domain, no professional email.

### Production — minimal

Add a domain (~$12–15/yr via Namecheap) and professional email.

| Addition | Option A | Option B |
|---|---|---|
| Domain | Namecheap (~$12–15/yr) | Squarespace Domains (~$20/yr) |
| Email | Namecheap Private Email (~$24/yr) | Google Workspace Starter (~$72/yr) |
| Hosting | GitHub Pages (free) | Vercel Hobby (free) |
| CMS | Pages CMS or Decap (free) | Same |
| Booking | Calendly free | Calendly Standard (~$120/yr) if needed |

**Estimated total: ~$36–50/yr** (domain + basic email)

### Production — premium visual editing

Replace Pages CMS / Decap with CloudCannon for a Squarespace-grade visual editor. CloudCannon pricing is materially higher — check <https://cloudcannon.com/pricing/> for current rates.

---

## Notes

- **Google Domains is no longer a standalone registrar.** Google acquired / migrated its domain customers to Squarespace. Use Namecheap or Squarespace Domains instead.
- **Vercel Hobby** is free forever for personal projects and supports private repos (unlike GitHub Pages free tier).
- **Calendly** downgrades to a limited free tier after the trial. The embed works on the free tier for basic scheduling.
