Brand

Warm Editorial Academic

The site should feel serious, thoughtful, humane, literate, editorial, and quietly premium. The closest archetype is an academic journal or essay magazine — not a coaching funnel, startup landing page, or creative portfolio.

Anti-goals

What this site should never be

PatternWhy it's wrong for Agreni
Translucent glass sections + backdrop-blur Reads as startup / app UI. Undermines editorial credibility.
3 animated color blobs at high opacity Feels designed in the wrong way — decorative-first, not content-first.
SaaS-style testimonial blocks with stars / photos Wrong register entirely. Academic credibility ≠ product reviews.
Icon-heavy service cards Agency / coaching funnel pattern. Weakens intellectual seriousness.
Dramatic scroll animations on every block Performative. Editorial sites earn attention through content, not motion.
"Passionate changemaker" copy LinkedIn filler. Agreni's voice is reflective, precise, and values-driven.
Bright palette / gradient hero Color-driven design is the wrong register for this brand.
Carousels by default Hides content. Static layouts perform better for reading and trust.
